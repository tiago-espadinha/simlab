SimLab - Physics & Visual Sandbox

A few self-contained canvas simulations

Spring-Mass Cloth

Verlet-integrated cloth you can drag, swing, and tear with the mouse.

2D Ripple

Click a height field to send waves rippling across the surface.

Double Pendulum

Deterministic chaos from two linked pendulum arms.

Falling Sand

Cellular sand, water, and walls you paint and watch settle.

Reaction-Diffusion

Gray-Scott chemistry producing organic, coral-like patterns.

N-Body / Solar System

Mutual gravity between bodies with real orbital velocities and trails.

Game of Life

Conway's classic cellular automaton with drawing and preset patterns.

Boids

Simulated flocking behavior of birds or fish.

Particle Gravity

Drop attractors and watch particles orbit under inverse-square gravity.
